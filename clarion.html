Clarion Bella Casa

Stay hungry, Stay foolish

About the restaurants

The Pink City now has a new address - Clarion Bella Casa. It is a full service business hotel in Jaipur. The hotel embodies the vibrant colors and the true spirit of Rajasthan. Located near the commercial hub of the city, the hotel is just 5 minutes away from the airport and 15 minutes away from the railway station.
Location
Clarion Bella Casa is positioned on Ashram Marg in Jaipur. Ayappa Mandir (Approx. 2km) and Shwetambar Jain Dadabari (Approx. 5km) are the places of interest easily accessible from the hotel. More exciting places like Sindhi Camp, Johri Bazaar, C-Scheme, Amer Fort, Hawa Mahal, Birla Temple, Nahargarh Fort, Jaigarh Fort, Jalmahal can also be visited during the stay.
Distances from Sanganer Jaipur Airport: Approx. 2km
Distances from Jaipur Railway Station: Approx. 12km
Hotel Facilities
The vast range of essential services offered for the comfort of the guests include room service, business center, gym, 24-hour front desk, air conditioning, beauty salon, conference facilities, laundry, parking, travel desk, 24-hour security. Additional features like body treatments, catering services, concierge, Jacuzzi, lounge, massage services, sauna, steam bath, banquet facilities can also be availed by the guests. The dining options include Zodiac, a 24 hours coffee shop and Fortune Deli serving pizzas, pastries, burgers etc. The hotel also houses a discotheque NOS. Neptune Bar is perfect to indulge in quality time with family and friends while enjoying drinks and snacks.
Rooms
Clarion Bella Casa offers 109 well-appointed rooms. All the rooms have been complimented with an elegant decor to match the taste of a modern day traveler. The spacious rooms feature a workstation, LCD television, mini bar and in-room safe, keeping in mind the comfort of the guests.
