Radisson Jaipur City Center

The best among all

About the hotel.

Radisson Jaipur City Center situated in the world heritage city with its majestic pink hues and charming forts & palaces has been a part of the fabled Golden Triangle. The 5 star deluxe hotel is ideal for both business and leisure stays. Opening up a new world with beautifully renovated hotel rooms, lobby and boasting of majestically luxurious suites the Radisson Jaipur City Center is strategically located in the heart of the city. The property offers prime amenities like concierge service, business travel desk, award winning restaurants, gym & spa, lounge, bar & nightclub and an outdoor pool. With major tourist attractions of the city in close proximity, the hotel is ideal to visit city's historic monuments like Hawa Mahal, Jantar Mantar, City Palace and Amber Fort or go about one's business schedule with comfortable ease as the major business hubs of the city, C-Scheme, Civil Lines, High Court, Circuit House & the Secretariat along with the railway station, the metro service and the bus stand are within close proximity to the hotel. The Jaipur Airport too is just 25 minutes away.

Rooms: Each of the Radisson Jaipur City Center newly renovated rooms and Radisson suites features smart dcor and first-rate fixtures to ensure a pleasant stay for guests. With free high-speed internet access, guests can complete travel arrangements from the comfort of their rooms.
