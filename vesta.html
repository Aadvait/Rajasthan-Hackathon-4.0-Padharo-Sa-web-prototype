Vesta International

Your dream food court

About the restaurants

Vesta International, Jaipur is a luxury hotel ideal for both business and leisure travellers. It offers well-equipped gymnasium and swimming pool for guests. In addition, the banqueting facilities are perfect to hold events and functions.
Location:
Vesta International is located at Linking Road, near Ajmer flyover, close to the railway station and bus stand. Local attractions like Hawa Mahal, City Palace are in proximity to the hotel. Jaipur International Airport: 12 km (approx.)
Jaipur Railway Station: 800 mts (approx.)
Hotel features:
Vesta International offers elegant interiors with fine furnishings. The number of facilities for the comfort of the guests include internet, business centre, gym, pool, 24-hour front desk and room service, air conditioning, conference facilities, elevators, laundry, parking, travel desk, 24-hour security, health club, banquet facilities, doctor on call, audio visual equipment and more. The hotel also houses 'The Pavilion' which is a 24 hour multi cuisine restaurant serving breakfast, lunch and dinner, 'Rice Bowl' specializing in Oriental cuisine, The Den which is a bar and bakery which provides finest cakes, pastries, muffins, croissants, breads and much more.
Rooms:
Guests can enjoy their stay at the hotel and make use of the range of amenities like air conditioning, internet access, mini bar, private bathroom, safe, telephone, bath tub, bedside lamp, direct dialing, flat-screen television, in-room menu, laundry bag, luggage space, tea/coffee maker and writing desk.
